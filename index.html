What is the blockchain and bitcoin?


Blockchain is a decentralized, transparent ledger
system where data is stored in blocks linked together,
creating a secure and immutable chain. Bitcoin, the
most renowned cryptocurrency, operates on this
blockchain technology, allowing peer-to-peer
transactions without a central authority. Introduced in
2009 Bitcoin uses a proof-of-work system to validate
and record transactions on the blockchain, ensuring
security and decentralization.
